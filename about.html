Hello, World!

Hi, I'm Sarah Kaydence.

I'm a multi-hatted creative, born and bred in Singapore.

My creative expertise encompasses both analog and digital mediums, such as illustration, print design and web design. The works I create take inspiration from both geek culture, and the unique forms of natural world fauna — introducing a playful yet meaningful take on what we know, and what could be.

A graphic designer by training and self-taught illustrator/musician — I've worked in creative studios, start-ups and global-sized companies from various industries for the past 8 years (and counting!) I'm also currently serving as a designer and music director/guitarist in my local church's creative and worship ministries.
