The City - Camp XL

Graphic Design

Camp XL was the name for my home church's annual church camp in mid 2019.

When I first heard of the proposed name, the first thought that came to my mind was the concept of upsizing in fast food culture. Hence, for the art direction — I decided to go with a 60/70's vintage diner aesthetic for all camp theming & marketing paraphernalia.

Below are some of the assets created by me for the camp, which include — guerilla marketing "order chits", camp booklet and camper & committee tags.
